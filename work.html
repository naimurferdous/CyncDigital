TikTok Shop Affiliate Program Framework

A complete creator-commerce affiliate framework built for TikTok Shop sellers — designed and self-tested, not yet run for a paying client.

  • ▸Built around the 24-hour window: most TikTok Shop purchases happen within 24 hours of a creator post, so onboarding and briefing are designed to hit that window from day one
  • ▸Segments creators into three types — Awareness (reach), Conversion (5K–100K niche, high-trust), and Multiplier (brings other creators) — and recruits accordingly rather than chasing follower count
  • ▸Day 1/3/7 onboarding sequence: single-action welcome on Day 1, a genuine performance check-in on Day 3, and a low-pressure content conversation on Day 7
  • ▸Tiered commission ladder — 8–12% CPA for new creators, up to +2–3% for consistent performers, and Hybrid/exclusivity terms for top creators
  • ▸Tracks leading indicators (time-to-first-post, 30-day retention) instead of vanity metrics like views

60-Day Fintech/CFD Affiliate Growth Strategy

A verified, source-checked strategy across five regulated fintech/CFD programs — eToro, Wise, IBKR, Plus500, and Robinhood — built to correct the bad information affiliate managers usually work from.

  • ▸Verified real cookie windows against primary sources instead of third-party blogs — e.g. Wise's actual window is 30 days, not the "365-day lifetime" commonly advertised
  • ▸Mapped how MiFID II regulation forced EU/UK entities from CPA+RevShare to CPA-only ($150–$800 flat per trader), while some non-EU entities still offer RevShare
  • ▸Flagged payout traps most managers miss — e.g. IBKR's 12–14 month payout delay from a year-long balance-holding requirement
  • ▸Structured as a 4-phase, 60-day rollout: Audit & Foundation (days 1–15) → Recruit with Intent (16–30) → Optimize & Tighten (31–45) → Report & Handover (46–60)
  • ▸Targets conservative, measurable outcomes for the 60-day window: +40% net active partners, +5pp FTD rate improvement, <2% reconciliation error across 4 tracking platforms, 48-hour fraud review SLA

Figures above are the strategy's built-in targets, not results from a completed paid engagement.
